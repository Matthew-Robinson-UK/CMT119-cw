Pattern Recognition

Pattern recognition uses the information gained through the previous process to identify similarities within the different sub tasks. Pattern recognition allows the effective solutions of one problem to be adapted and applied to another. A recent example of pattern recognition would be in the creation of the Covid-19 vaccine through identifying similarities in the spike protein receptors (Corey et al. 2020).

In our website we can use the information and resources gained from producing a horizontal navigation bar to similarly produce a vertical sidebar.
